Time: 4PM - 6PM

LOGISTICS

Every group will have their own designated area, and will be set up similarly to a drive-in movie. You can watch the concert through your windshield and tune in with your radio. You can turn the car around, pop the trunk and watch through the back, or even just set up your own chairs!
